Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies on the collection and use of your information when you use The Digital Junk Drawer.

Open Source Note: This is a community-driven project. Applications within this "Junk Drawer" are contributed by various developers via GitHub. While we review contributions, each individual app may have its own behavior.

1. Data Collection

We do not require user accounts, and we do not manually collect personally identifiable information (like your name or phone number) just for browsing the site.

Usage Data

Because this website is hosted on GitHub Pages, GitHub may collect certain log data (such as IP addresses and browser types) for security and analytics purposes. This is managed by GitHub, not by us.

2. Community Contributions

If you choose to contribute an app to this project via GitHub:

  • Your GitHub profile and the code you submit will be public.
  • We require contributors to ensure their apps do not contain malicious trackers or hidden data-collection scripts.

3. Cookies

The core website does not use tracking cookies for marketing. However, individual games or apps within the drawer may use "Local Storage" or "Session Cookies" to save your high scores or app preferences locally on your device.

4. Links to Other Sites

Our service contains links to other websites (like GitHub, LinkedIn, or contributor portfolios). We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy policies of any third-party sites.

5. Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We review all community code submissions to ensure they are safe for our users.
